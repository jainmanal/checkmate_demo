A GAME WITHOUT A GAME

PLAY-TO-COLLECT

The objective of the collectible card is to collect a set of 6 cards. The rarity each set will determine the rewards that are unlocked.

DIGITAL COLLECTIBLES

Each Checkmate card is a limited edition blockchain collectible minted to a non-fungible token. Checkmate's commissioned artwork is authentic and scarce - it can only be earned, won or traded but not reproduced.

UNIQUE AND RARE

Non-fungible tokens (NFTs) are unique and verifiable proofs of authenticity and ownership within a blockchain network. NFT's are not interchangeable with each other and introduce a scarcity to the digital world.
